Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Personal Tarot Reader

Since Personal Tarot Reader stores all data locally on your device, deleting the app will remove all associated data.

iOS

  1. Press and hold the Personal Tarot Reader app icon
  2. Tap "Remove App" or "Delete App"
  3. Confirm deletion

Android

  1. Go to Settings > Apps
  2. Find and select Personal Tarot Reader
  3. Tap "Uninstall"

Export Before Deleting

If you wish to keep a record of your readings, use the Export feature in the app before deleting:

  1. Open Personal Tarot Reader
  2. Go to Settings > Export Data
  3. Choose your preferred format (PDF or JSON)
  4. Save the file to your device or cloud storage

Coloring With Friends

Coloring With Friends stores data both locally and on our servers.

Delete Account and All Data

  1. Open Coloring With Friends
  2. Go to Settings > Account
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision

This will permanently delete:

  • Your account and profile
  • All saved artwork
  • Collaboration history
  • All personal data

Export Before Deleting

To save your artwork before deletion:

  1. Go to Gallery
  2. Select the artwork you want to save
  3. Tap Export and choose format (PNG, SVG)

Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app to delete your account, you can request deletion by email:

  1. Email us at privacy@tkchi.studio
  2. Include your username and the email associated with your account
  3. State that you wish to delete your account and all associated data
  4. We will process your request within 30 days

Data Retention After Deletion

After you delete your account:

  • Your data is immediately removed from active systems
  • Backup copies are deleted within 90 days
  • Anonymous analytics data may be retained (cannot be linked to you)

Contact

If you have questions about data deletion, please contact us.
