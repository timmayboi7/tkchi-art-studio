Personal Tarot Reader

Your private digital sanctuary

Overview

Personal Tarot Reader is designed for those who value privacy in their spiritual practice. Unlike other tarot apps, we don't require accounts, don't store your readings in the cloud, and don't track your behavior. Everything stays on your device.

Features

True Privacy

No accounts. No cloud storage. No data collection. Your readings are yours alone.

Multiple Spreads

Single card, three-card, Celtic cross, and custom spreads for every question.

Journal

Record your interpretations and reflections. Stored locally, encrypted on device.

Offline First

Works without internet. Your practice isn't dependent on connectivity.

Beautiful Art

Original industrial-minimalist card art designed specifically for this app.

Export & Backup

Export your journal as PDF or JSON. You control your data.
