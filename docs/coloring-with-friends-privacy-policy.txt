PRIVACY POLICY - COLORING WITH FRIENDS
TKChi Art Studio
Last Updated: February 16, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy explains how Coloring With Friends collects, uses, and protects 
your information when you use our collaborative coloring application.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Account Information:
- Username (chosen by you)
- Optional: Email address for account recovery

Content:
- Drawings and artwork created in the app
- Canvas data during active sessions

Technical Information:
- Device type and operating system
- App version
- Anonymous usage statistics

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

- To provide real-time collaboration features
- To save your artwork to your account
- To improve app performance and features
- To communicate important updates

4. DATA SHARING

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data:
- With other participants in your shared canvases
- With service providers who assist our operations
- If required by law

5. DATA RETENTION

Your artwork is stored as long as you maintain your account. Temporary session 
data is deleted after 30 days of inactivity.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Export your artwork
- Opt out of non-essential communications

7. SECURITY

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. 
However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Coloring With Friends is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly 
collect information from children under 13.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant 
changes through the app or email.

10. CONTACT US

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
Email: privacy@tkchi.studio
Web: https://tkchi.studio/legal/contact.html
