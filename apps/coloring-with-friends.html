Coloring With Friends

Create together, anywhere

Overview

Coloring With Friends brings people together through collaborative art. Join a shared canvas with friends or family and create in real-time. Perfect for long-distance connections, creative hangouts, or quiet moments of shared focus.

Features

Real-Time Collaboration

See strokes appear instantly as friends draw. Smooth, low-latency sync.

Shared Canvases

Create public or private rooms. Invite via link or code.

Rich Toolset

Brushes, pencils, markers, and fill tools. Layer support for complex pieces.

Templates Library

Start from blank or choose from hundreds of coloring templates.

Export & Share

Save as PNG, SVG, or animated timelapse. Share your creations.

Cross-Platform

iOS, Android, and web. Draw on your tablet, phone, or desktop.

Built for Connection

Whether you're looking for a creative activity during a video call, a way to stay connected with distant friends, or a collaborative tool for art therapy, Coloring With Friends adapts to your needs.

  • Family game nights across time zones
  • Creative team building for remote teams
  • Art therapy sessions with therapists
  • Classroom activities for remote learning
  • Quiet companionship without the pressure of conversation
