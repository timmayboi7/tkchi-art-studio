PRIVACY POLICY - PERSONAL TAROT READER
TKChi Art Studio
Last Updated: February 16, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

TKChi Art Studio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. 
This Privacy Policy explains how Personal Tarot Reader handles your information.

KEY POINT: We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers.

2. INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

We do NOT collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, address)
- Tarot readings or interpretations
- Journal entries
- Usage data or analytics
- Device identifiers
- Location data

3. DATA STORAGE

All data created in Personal Tarot Reader is stored LOCALLY ON YOUR DEVICE only. 
This includes:
- Your tarot readings
- Journal entries
- App preferences

4. DATA SECURITY

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. On supported 
devices, app data may be encrypted using hardware-level encryption.

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Personal Tarot Reader does not integrate with any third-party services, 
analytics, or advertising networks.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Personal Tarot Reader is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly 
collect any information from anyone.

7. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any 
changes by posting the new policy on this page.
